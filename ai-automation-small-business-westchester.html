How to Automate Customer Follow-Up for Small Business

The highest-ROI automation for most local businesses is the follow-up sequence. Here's how it typically works:

  1. Customer fills out a contact form, texts, or emails
  2. AI sends an instant, personalized reply acknowledging their inquiry
  3. If no response in 24 hours, AI sends a gentle follow-up
  4. If still no response, AI flags the lead for a human call

A Westchester home services business running this workflow typically converts 20-35% more leads than one relying on manual follow-up — simply because speed-to-response is the single biggest factor in whether a lead converts.
